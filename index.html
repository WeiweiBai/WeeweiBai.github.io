Economic

As a student at the University of California, Riverside (UCR), I immersed myself in the dynamic field of economics and developed a keen interest in the complex workings of the business world. Majoring in economics was a deliberate choice because I have an insatiable curiosity about how markets work and how businesses work. Over the course of my academic career, I have delved deeply into various disciplines of economics, from microeconomic theory to macroeconomic policy, from econometrics to behavioral economics. Each course broadened my intellectual horizons, improved my analytical skills, and provided me with the tools I needed to understand complex economic phenomena and form informed opinions. Studying Economics at UCR has not only provided me with a rigorous academic framework, but also a wealth of practical applications and opportunities to participate in the real world. I actively participate in case competitions, working with peers to address business challenges and develop strategic solutions. These experiences have not only honed my problem-solving skills, but also cultivated my tenacious entrepreneurial spirit.

I am a skilled communicator and interpersonal person, able to effectively deal with people from different backgrounds and at different levels, build strong relationships and promote seamless collaboration within the team. I am proficient in Microsoft Office software, good at using Excel to analyze and process complex data, obtain valuable insights and make wise decisions. I have a strong interest in the field of business and marketing, and driven by this interest, I constantly explore innovative business models and strategies. With a growth mindset, I am eager to constantly expand my knowledge and skills, embrace emerging trends and technologies, and stay ahead of the curve in an ever-changing environment. My passion for entrepreneurship is complemented by my commitment to excellence, and I am constantly looking for opportunities to challenge myself, broaden my horizons, and make a meaningful contribution to the dynamic business world.

In the campus community, I am an enthusiastic member of the economic community, acting as an activity planner and organizer. Through careful planning and execution, I spearheaded the organization of a series of engaging events that greatly increased the club's impact. From thought-provoking seminars to interactive workshops, each event not only attracts a different audience, but also fosters meaningful discussion and networking opportunities within the field of economics. In addition, I actively participate in campus business competitions, applying my analytical skills and strategic acumen to tackle complex challenges. These experiences not only honed my business analysis and problem-solving skills, but also earned me accolades and recognition, including "the ability to thrive in a competitive environment." In addition to extracurricular activities, I also collaborate with my classmates on small-scale entrepreneurship, leveraging our collective creativity and ingenuity to turn innovative ideas into reality. From concept to execution, these projects have provided me with valuable hands-on experience, allowing me to develop practical business insights and hone my management skills in a real-world environment. I can also speak fluent Japanese, Chinese and English.
